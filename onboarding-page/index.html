Welcome note from Gurnoor

Welcome onboard, Guardians!

I’m very excited to have you join the team!
There’s a lot to learn in these first weeks, but I wanted to take a minute to start with the heart of the matter — our company core values. At GOTOKO, these values guide everything we do: Customer obsessed - Obsessed about GOTOKO’s customers and their experience Ownership mindset - Leads by example and goes the extra mile to help GOTOKO succeed Agile - Committed to explore, new ways of working and adapts to new situations and challenges for GOTOKO’s betterment Collaborate with Compassion - Creates an inclusive, team-oriented and supportive environment and always puts GOTOKO first They’re what makes us GOTOKO. As you become part of the team, I’m excited about how they’ll shape your professional growth and experience, too.
GOTOKO is a product & technology company and our biggest asset is not our source code or our platform. It is every single Guardian, person, and stakeholder who sacrifices so much every single day in the united belief of making an impact. It’s not the tool or technology, but it's the people who use it and nurture it which make the difference. This belief has proven right in the unbelievable resilience Guardians have shown in extremely challenging circumstances. Things will get tough, especially in the uncertain world we live in. As always it will be your strength, passion, and resilience which will get us through impossible odds.
Welcome to GOTOKO once again and #MajuBersamaGOTOKO!
